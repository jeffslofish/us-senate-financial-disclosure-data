Annual Report for 2018 Due Date Extension 1

Ladda Tammy Duckworth requested an extension of time for filing the annual Public Financial Disclosure Report. The report was originally due on May 15, 2019.

Pursuant to section 101(g) of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978, as amended, a 30-day extension was granted and the new due date for the report is no later than June 14, 2019.
